# Text Box

Text Box lets you place your own text anywhere on a page — a note, a label, a
fill-in on a form that doesn't have real fields.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Text box**, from the toolbar or the **Edit** tools.
2. Click where you want the text.
3. Type it, then press **Enter** to place it (**Esc** to cancel).

## What to know

- **It's held until you Save,** then written into the document as real text.
- **Reorganizing pages clears unsaved text.** If you rotate, delete, or reorder
  pages before saving, text you haven't saved is cleared — save first.
