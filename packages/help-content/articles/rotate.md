# Rotate Pages

Rotate Pages turns the pages you've selected 90° clockwise — for a page that was
scanned sideways, or a landscape exhibit in a portrait document.

## How to do it

1. Select the page or pages you want to turn (in the thumbnail rail on the left,
   or in **Organize Pages**).
2. In the **Organize** tools, choose **Rotate Pages**.

Each time you use it, the selected pages turn another quarter-turn clockwise.

## What to know

- **It changes the open document.** Save to keep it — use **Save As** to keep the
  original unrotated.
- **Select first.** If no pages are selected, nothing happens.
- To turn several pages while seeing the whole document, work in
  **Organize Pages** instead.
