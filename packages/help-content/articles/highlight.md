# Highlight

Highlight marks text the way a yellow marker would — and it saves as a real
highlight in the PDF, so anyone who opens the file sees it.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Highlight**, from the toolbar or the **Edit** tools.
2. Drag across the text you want to mark.
3. To remove one, click a highlight you've added.

## What to know

- **Highlights are held until you Save,** then written into the document as real
  annotations (not baked into the page).
- **It needs real text to grab onto.** On a scanned page, run **Make Searchable**
  first so there's text to highlight.
- **Reorganizing pages clears unsaved highlights.** If you rotate, delete, or
  reorder pages before saving, highlights you haven't saved are cleared — save
  first.
