Draw

Draw lets you make freehand marks — circle something, add an arrow, initial a spot. When you save, the mark becomes a real PDF annotation.

How to do it

  1. Choose Draw, from the toolbar or the Edit tools.
  2. Pick a color or line width if you want a different mark.
  3. Drag on the page to draw.

What to know

  • It's held until you Save, then saved as a PDF annotation.
  • Print markup controls printing. Leave Print markup on to include saved ink when printing, or turn it off before saving new ink.
  • Flatten markup makes it permanent. Use Flatten markup when you want the ink baked into the page.
  • Prepare for Filing asks first. If saved RaioPDF markup is present, filing offers to flatten it or keep it live before continuing.
  • Reorganizing pages clears unsaved ink. Rotate, delete, or reorder pages before saving and it's cleared — save first.
