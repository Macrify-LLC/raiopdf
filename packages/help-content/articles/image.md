# Image

Image puts a PNG or JPEG picture onto a page — a logo, a stamp, a photo.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Image**, from the toolbar or the **Edit** tools.
2. Pick a PNG or JPEG file.
3. Click the page where you want it.

## What to know

- **PNG and JPEG only.**
- **It's held until you Save,** then written into the document.
- **Drag to move it; the corner handles resize it.** Select it first.
- **Pin it to lock it.** Click the pin badge in the corner to lock the image in
  place — a pinned image can't be moved and lets clicks pass through to whatever's
  underneath. Click the pin again to unpin.
- **Remove it with the ×.** An unpinned image shows a small × next to its pin;
  click it to delete. Unpin first to reveal the ×.
- **Reorganizing pages clears an unsaved image.** Rotate, delete, or reorder pages
  before saving and it's cleared — save first.
- To add a picture as its own full page instead of placing it on one, use
  **Insert images as pages**.
