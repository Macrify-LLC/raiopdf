# Sign

Sign puts your signature on the page. Draw it right there, or use a signature
image you've saved before.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Sign**, from the toolbar or the **Edit** tools — the **Signature**
   card opens.
2. **Draw** your signature, or choose **Image from file** to upload one.
3. Click **Use Signature**, then click the page to place it.

## What to know

- **Your saved signatures stay on this computer** — they aren't uploaded anywhere.
- **Flatten on save** is on by default, which bakes the signature into the page
  when you Save — it becomes part of the page rather than a removable layer. That's
  usually what you want on a signed document.
- **It's held until you Save.** Reorganizing pages before saving clears a
  signature you've placed but not yet saved — save first.
