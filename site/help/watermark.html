Watermark

Watermark lays repeating text — DRAFT, CONFIDENTIAL, or your own wording — across the pages so it shows on every one.

How to do it

  1. In the Edit tools, choose Watermark....
  2. Tap DRAFT or CONFIDENTIAL, or type your own Text.
  3. Set the Pages, the Direction (Diagonal or Horizontal), and the Opacity (how faint or bold it is).
  4. Click Apply Watermark.

What to know

  • The text stamps into the page — it's part of the page, not a removable note.
  • Applying updates the open document right away, but it's only written to your file when you Save. Use Save As to keep a clean original.
