# Insert images as pages

Have a photo or a scan sitting as a PNG or JPEG? Insert images as pages turns each
image into a full PDF page and adds them into your document.

## How to do it

1. In the **Organize** tools, choose **Insert images as pages...**.
2. Under **Images**, choose one or more PNG or JPEG files.
3. Click **Insert Images**.

The images go in at the first page you've selected, or at the current page.

## What to know

- **PNG and JPEG only.**
- **The result is a new file.** When you **Save**, RaioPDF asks for a new file
  name, so your original is left untouched.
- An inserted image is a picture, not text. To make it searchable, run
  **Make Searchable** afterward.
