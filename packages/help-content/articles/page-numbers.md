# Page Numbers

Page Numbers adds numbering to the pages — plain "1, 2, 3" or "Page 1 of 10" —
stamped onto the page.

## How to do it

1. In the **Edit** tools, choose **Page Numbers...**.
2. Set the **Pages**, the **Start at** number, the **Font size**, the **Format**
   ("1, 2, 3" or "Page N of M"), and the **Position** (header or footer, left,
   center, or right).
3. Click **Apply Page Numbers**.

## What to know

- **The numbers stamp into the page** — they're part of the page, not a removable
  note.
- **Applying updates the open document right away,** but it's only written to your
  file when you **Save**. Use **Save As** to keep an un-numbered original.
