# Comment

Leave notes on a document by dropping a pin on the page and typing your note.
When you save, the notes become real PDF annotations — the kind a reviewer sees
in any PDF program — not marks baked into the page.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Comment**, from the toolbar or the **Annotate** group in the Tools
   panel. Your saved comments are listed in the same group.
2. Click the spot on the page where you want the note. A little box opens.
3. Type your note and click **Save Note** (or press **Enter**; use
   **Shift+Enter** for a new line, **Esc** to cancel).
4. To change or remove a note later, click its pin.

## What to know

- **Comments are real annotations.** They stay as notes attached to the document,
  which anyone can open, reply to, or clear in their own PDF program — they aren't
  flattened into the page.
- **They're pending until you save.** A note you've dropped is held in the app and
  written into the document when you **Save**.
- **Reorganizing pages clears unsaved notes.** If you rotate, delete, or reorder
  pages while you have unsaved comments, those pending notes are cleared — so save
  your comments first.
