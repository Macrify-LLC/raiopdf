# Merge PDFs

Merge PDFs puts other documents after the one you have open, in the order you
choose, and gives you back a single combined file.

## How to do it

1. Open the PDF you want first — it stays first.
2. In the **Organize** tools, choose **Merge PDFs...**.
3. Click **Add PDFs...** and choose the files.
4. They're listed after the current one, in order.
5. Click the **Merge Files** button — it shows the count (for example,
   *Merge 3 Files*).

## What to know

- **The result is a new file.** When you **Save**, RaioPDF asks for a new file
  name, so your original documents are left untouched.
- **The current PDF stays first;** the ones you add follow in the order shown.
