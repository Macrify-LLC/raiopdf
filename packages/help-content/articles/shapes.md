# Shapes

Shapes let you mark a page with rectangles, ellipses, straight lines, and
arrows that save into the PDF.

## How to do it

1. Choose **Rectangle**, **Ellipse**, **Line**, or **Arrow** from the toolbar or
   the **Edit** tools.
2. Pick a stroke color, stroke width, and fill color if the shape supports one.
3. Drag on the page to size and place the shape.

## What to know

- **It's held until you Save,** then written into the document.
- **Fill is optional.** Rectangle and ellipse can stay stroke-only.
- **Reorganizing pages clears unsaved shapes.** Rotate, delete, or reorder pages
  before saving and they're cleared — save first.
